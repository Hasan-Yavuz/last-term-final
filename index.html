Our Green Commitment

At The Wheel Depot Plus we care about the environment. This is the reason we continuously promote the used tire industry. The idea of used tires is misunderstood to be a risk in terms of the quality of the product. We aim to educate people about the ways this has been combated - including quality-assurance measures such as our practice of only selling used tires with at least 50% remaining tread life.
